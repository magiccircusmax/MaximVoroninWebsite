Directing/Consultation

Act Creation & Show Development with Maxim Voronin

Maxim Voronin has directed and refined a wide range of shows and acts for artists worldwide. Whether you're an artist struggling to develop a compelling act or a producer seeking to create a high-quality show, Maxim is here to help.

With expertise in choreography, pantomime, and acting, Maxim will guide you in shaping unique ideas and elevating your performance. He also provides support in technical aspects such as engineering, costume design, lighting, and music selection to ensure every detail enhances your act.

Already have an act or show but want to take it to the next level? Maxim offers consulting services to help integrate new effects, props, and illusions. For example, if you envision a dramatic disappearance effect in your show, he will work with you to conceptualize the illusion, connect you with the right prop designers, determine a fair budget, and seamlessly integrate it into your performance with precision and professionalism.

If you're interested in working with Maxim, whether in-person or online, send an email to magiccircusmax@gmail.com outlining your goals. A free 30-minute consultation will be scheduled to discuss your vision, develop a plan of action, and provide a personalized quote.
